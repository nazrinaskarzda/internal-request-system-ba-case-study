# Internal Request System - Business Analysis Case Study

## Overview

This project presents a full end-to-end business analysis lifecycle for designing an internal request management system (MIS). It demonstrates how business needs are identified, analyzed, structured into requirements, and validated through testing and evaluation.

---

## Business Problem

Internal service requests are currently handled through manual and fragmented processes, leading to delays, lack of transparency, and inefficient communication across departments.

---

## Business Objectives

* Improve request processing efficiency
* Increase transparency and tracking
* Standardize internal workflows
* Enable better decision-making through data

---

## Solution Overview

A centralized internal request management system that allows employees to submit, track, and manage service requests digitally.

---

## BA Lifecycle Approach

### 1. Initiation

* Problem Statement & Business Need
* Stakeholder Identification & Analysis

* RACI Matrix
* Assumptions Log
* Risk Log

---

### 2. Planning & Analysis

* Elicitation & Communication Planning
* Current State Analysis (AS-IS)
* Business & Stakeholder Requirements
* Requirements Traceability
* Business → Stakeholder → Functional → NFR Mapping

---

### 3. Solution Design

* Business Requirements (BR)
* Stakeholder Requirements (SR)
* Functional Requirements (FR)
* Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)
* Context Diagram
* Data Flow Diagram (DFD)
* User Flow
* Use Case Diagram
* BPMN Process

---

### 4. Development Support

* Requirement Clarifications
* Change Request Handling
* Requirement Updates
* Stakeholder Collaboration

---

### 5. Testing & Validation

* UAT Planning
* Test Cases
* Acceptance Criteria
* Error Scenarios
* SLA Definitions

---

### 6. Evaluation & Measurement

* KPI Definition
* Success Metrics
* Post-Implementation Evaluation
